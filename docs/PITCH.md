# NEXUS — Pitch Assets

Everything you say, from the elevator to the boardroom. One product, one buyer, one story.

---

## 10-second value proposition
> **Ask your strategy a question. Get an answer that remembers, verifies, and improves.**

## 30-second pitch
> Enterprise strategy teams make nine-figure bets on assumptions nobody writes down. When
> a bet fails, no one can say which belief broke or what else was standing on it. NEXUS
> builds a **Decision Twin**: it captures every decision and its assumptions, seals each on
> the record before the outcome exists, and verifies it against reality. When an assumption
> is falsified, the twin propagates that one fact across the whole strategy — dropping
> confidence, re-ranking what to change, and learning. ChatGPT answers and forgets. The
> Decision Twin remembers, verifies, and compounds.

## The one-line positioning
> The decision twin enterprise strategy teams can actually check.

## The aha, scripted
> **CEO:** Why did we fail in Brazil — and what else is now at risk?
>
> **Decision Twin:** The Brazil launch rested on one belief — *demand grows >18% YoY.*
> Reality settled it false: ~6%. Four other decisions stood on that same belief; their
> confidence just dropped. Ranked by impact: hold Mexico, pause the São Paulo center,
> reprice LATAM, freeze the sales backfill. The EU platform and the sales-motion change
> don't depend on this belief — untouched. And the twin learned: this assumption's
> falsification rate rose, so every future strategy leaning on it now starts lower.

## The investor case (Series A)

- **Category:** decision intelligence for the enterprise strategy office — a budget that
  exists, an owner who is accountable, a pain that recurs every quarter.
- **Wedge:** the verified decision record — sealed before the outcome, checkable by a
  stranger. No incumbent and no foundation model can produce it retroactively.
- **Moat:** a compounding corpus of verified enterprise decisions + the organizational
  memory on top. Copy the UI in a week; you cannot copy three years of sealed outcomes.
- **Flywheel:** more verified decisions → sharper twin → better recommendations → more
  usage → higher switching cost. Switching cost rises every quarter the customer stays.
- **Why now:** every enterprise has a general model and zero memory of whether its
  forecasts were right. NEXUS is the system of record for that.

## Why AWS, in one breath
> Bedrock reasons every decision. EventBridge schedules the verification. Lambda settles
> outcomes and runs the cascade with no human in the loop. DynamoDB is the Decision Graph.
> S3 Object Lock makes every seal write-once. CloudWatch audits it. Take any one away and a
> capability stops — that's why AWS is inevitable, not attached.
