# NEXUS — the 90-second demo

One product. One broken belief. The whole strategy re-scored in front of the judge.

| t | You do | You say | They see |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0:00 | Open `nexus-landing/standalone/decision-graph.html?api=…` | "Strategy teams bet hundreds of millions on assumptions nobody writes down. This is the **Decision Twin** — it wrote them down." | The question, the sealed belief, five confident decisions. |
| 0:15 | Point at the ember panel | "This belief — *Brazil demand grows >18%* — was **sealed 45 days before the outcome existed**. Reality just settled it: ~6%." | The belief, marked sealed-before-outcome. |
| 0:25 | Click **▶ Let reality settle it** | "Watch one falsified belief walk the graph." | Confidence drains, decision by decision; cards turn red. |
| 0:45 | Let it finish | "**$40M of committed capital just got repriced** — from one broken belief. And the two decisions that don't depend on it stayed untouched." | The `$40M` headline; controls correctly untouched. |
| 0:55 | Point at rank 1 | "Every recommendation reads like an exec memo: action, dollar impact, why now, evidence — and the **alternative** to take instead." | The ranked cards with `$24.1M / $10.1M / …` and "instead:". |
| 1:10 | Point at *the twin learned* | "The belief's falsification rate rose. Every **future** strategy that leans on it now starts lower — automatically." | The before→after learning stat. |
| 1:20 | (optional) open `verify.html` on a phone | "And you don't take our word for it — recompute the seal on your own phone." | Hash recomputed client-side; seal predates outcome. |

**The one line they remember:** *one broken belief, $40M of strategy re-priced in real time, and the twin got smarter.*

Runs fully offline on embedded data — a hostile venue network cannot break the centerpiece. Add `?api=http://localhost:8000` to drive it from the live engine.
